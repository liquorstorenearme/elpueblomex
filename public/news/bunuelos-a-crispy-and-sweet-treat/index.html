Buñuelos: A Crispy and Sweet Treat at El Pueblo

A fried tortilla, perfectly crispy on the outside and sprinkled with a generous coating of cinnamon and sugar — eight for $4.99.

If you're a fan of great Mexican food and have a sweet tooth, we have exciting news. El Pueblo is proud to introduce a new addition to the menu — Buñuelos. These crispy, sweet treats are bound to satisfy your cravings and leave you wanting more.

Buñuelos are a beloved dessert in Mexican culture, and we're thrilled to share this traditional delicacy with our customers. Imagine a fried tortilla, perfectly crispy on the outside and sprinkled with a generous coating of cinnamon and sugar. Each bite is a delightful combination of textures and flavors that will transport your taste buds straight to Mexico.

What makes our Buñuelos even more enticing is that you can enjoy a serving of 8 for just $4.99. It's an affordable indulgence that you won't want to miss. Whether you're dining in with family or grabbing a quick treat on the go, they're the perfect choice.

What are buñuelos?

Buñuelos are a traditional Mexican dessert that has been enjoyed for generations. They're made by flattening and stretching dough, similar to tortillas, and then deep-frying until golden-brown and crispy. The magic happens when they come out of the fryer and are immediately coated with cinnamon and sugar. That coating adds a sweet, aromatic layer that elevates the simple tortilla into a heavenly dessert.

Why you'll love ours

  • Perfect crispiness — fried to the ideal level, guaranteeing a satisfying crunch in every bite.
  • Generous cinnamon and sugar coating — sweet and fragrant, tantalizing on first taste.
  • Affordable treat — 8 for just $4.99, a real indulgence you can enjoy any time.
  • Traditional recipe — passed down through generations for an authentic taste.

How to enjoy them

  • On their own — crispy texture, sweet coating, enough to make your taste buds dance.
  • Dip and savor — pair with chocolate, caramel, or whipped cream for an extra layer of indulgence.
  • Ice cream topping — crumble over your favorite ice cream flavor for a crunchy, sweet twist.
