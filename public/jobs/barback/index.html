Responsibilities

  • Maintain cleanliness and organization of the bar area
  • Restock and replenish bar supplies, including glassware and ingredients
  • Assist bartenders in preparing and serving drinks
  • Collect and dispose of empty glasses and bottles efficiently
  • Ensure the bar is well-equipped and ready for peak service hours
  • Support bartenders with tasks to maintain smooth operational flow
  • Contribute to creating a positive and enjoyable atmosphere for customers

Requirements

  • Prior experience in a barback or hospitality role preferred
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
  • Physical stamina to lift supplies and stand for extended periods
  • Excellent teamwork and communication skills
  • Basic knowledge of bar equipment, glassware, and drink ingredients
  • Flexible schedule, including evenings, weekends, and holidays

Benefits

  • Health Insurance
  • One free meal during your shift
  • Sick Pay
  • Raises based on work performance
  • 401(K)
