Responsibilities

  • Lead and supervise a shift of 5-7 team members: cashier/server, line cook, prep, sanitation
  • Champion a spirit of teamwork and pride in quality customer service
  • Ensure guests receive kind, courteous, prompt service
  • Ensure food is prepared correctly with utmost quality
  • Maintain food safety standards
  • Bilingual — English/Spanish is a plus
  • Highly dependable and reliable
  • Flexible schedule including nights, weekends, holidays
  • Team-oriented leader, leads by example
  • Excellent problem-solving and reasoning
  • Operate a POS system and cash register
  • Authorized to work in the U.S.

Requirements

  • Food Handler's Certificate
  • 3-5 years of restaurant management experience

Benefits

  • Health Insurance
  • One free meal during your shift
  • Sick Pay
  • Raises based on work performance
  • 401(K)
