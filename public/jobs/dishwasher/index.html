Dishwasher

Join our dynamic team as a Restaurant Dishwasher. You'll play a crucial role in maintaining the cleanliness and organization of our kitchen — ensuring timely cleaning and sanitization of all dishes, utensils, and kitchen equipment.

Responsibilities

  • Wash and sanitize dishes, glassware, flatware, and kitchen equipment
  • Organize and stack cleaned dishes for quick access
  • Properly handle and store cleaned items
  • Remove trash and recyclables from kitchen and dining areas
  • Clean and sanitize kitchen surfaces and food prep areas
  • Support kitchen staff with food prep as needed
  • Comply with health and safety regulations
  • Work efficiently during peak hours
  • Maintain a positive, collaborative attitude
  • Report equipment malfunctions or maintenance needs

Requirements

  • Prior dishwashing experience a plus, not required
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Physical stamina to stand for long periods and lift heavy loads
  • Strong responsibility and attention to cleanliness
  • Excellent communication and teamwork
  • Flexibility for evenings, weekends, and holidays

Benefits

  • Health Insurance
  • One free meal during your shift
  • Sick Pay
  • Raises based on work performance
  • 401(K)
