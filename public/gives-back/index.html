Giving back to our community

El Pueblo Gives Back.

El Pueblo Mexican Food has consistently supported and contributed to the community. We're excited to partner with you through our fundraiser program — a simple, delicious way to raise funds. We donate 20% of net proceeds from your event to make a meaningful impact.

To ensure everything runs smoothly, please submit your fundraiser application at least one month before your desired event date.
