Full bar · Cocktails · Beer · Spirits

Two real full bars in North County.

Whiskey, bourbon, vodka, gin, rum — every classic spirit you'd expect, plus the tequila and mezcal selection you'd hope for at a Mexican kitchen. Cocktails, wine, beers on tap, and the best margaritas in North County. The Del Mar and Carmel Valley bars pour everything, all served alongside the same kitchen that runs fish tacos until midnight.

On the bar

What's pouring.

A Real Full Bar

Whiskey, bourbon, vodka, gin, rum, tequila, mezcal — every classic spirit, plus wine by the glass. Order anything you'd order at a real bar.

Cold Beer on Tap

Beers on tap at Del Mar — rotating local craft and Mexican imports. Bottles and cans at every location, always cold.

Cocktails & Margaritas

House margaritas pressed fresh — no syrupy mixes. Plus every classic cocktail done right: old fashioneds, palomas, mojitos, martinis.
