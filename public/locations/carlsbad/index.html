Mexican Food in Carlsbad — La Costa Town Square

El Pueblo Carlsbad sits in the heart of La Costa Town Square at 7720 Rancho Santa Fe Road, across from the shops and a short drive from South Carlsbad State Beach, La Costa Resort, and the Omni La Costa. Whether you're rolling in after a morning at the beach, grabbing dinner after errands at the Town Square shops, or feeding the family between kids' sports, our Carlsbad kitchen has you covered.

We're open early (6am for breakfast burritos) and late (until midnight on Friday and Saturday), with a full menu of the Mexican food that's made El Pueblo a North County institution. Counter-service seating inside, a parking lot outside, and the same fresh-every-day kitchen that feeds our other four locations.

Fish Tacos, Breakfast Burritos, and Family-Size Plates

Our Carlsbad menu is the full El Pueblo spread: the signature $1.39 fish taco (fried-to-order, chipotle sauce, cabbage, pico, all day), breakfast burritos from 6am (Super, Steak and Eggs, Machaca, Chorizo, classic Breakfast), enchiladas, carne asada plates, fajita plates, chile rellenos, tortas, quesadillas, and Mexican soups like pozole and chicken tortilla soup.

Feeding a crowd? Our party packs work from our Carlsbad kitchen — make-your-own taco packs, rolled tacos, enchiladas, quesadilla trays, and chips-and-salsa trays for gatherings of any size. Call ahead for catering, or order same-day for pickup.

Family-Friendly Dining in La Costa

La Costa families know us as the easy dinner answer — fast enough for a weeknight, cheap enough to feed four kids, and close enough to swing through after Alga Norte Park or Stagecoach. We have a kids menu (kids quesadilla, bean and cheese burrito, rice and beans), horchata and jamaica on tap, and no-fuss counter service so you're never stuck waiting for a check.

Frequently asked

Carlsbad — questions

Where is El Pueblo Carlsbad?

7720 Rancho Santa Fe Road, Carlsbad, CA 92009 — in La Costa Town Square, across from the shops and close to La Costa Resort.

What are El Pueblo Carlsbad's hours?

Sunday through Thursday we're open 6am to 10pm. Friday and Saturday we stay open until midnight. Breakfast menu starts at 6am daily.

Do you have a kids menu?

Yes. The Carlsbad location has a kids menu with kids quesadilla, bean and cheese burrito, and sides. Horchata and jamaica are on the drink menu.

Is there parking at La Costa Town Square?

Yes. La Costa Town Square has a large shared parking lot — no meters, easy access right off Rancho Santa Fe Road.

Do you cater in Carlsbad?

Yes. Our Carlsbad kitchen fulfills the full El Pueblo catering and party-pack menu — make-your-own taco packs, rolled tacos, enchilada trays, quesadilla trays, and chips-and-salsa trays. Call the store or submit a catering request online.
