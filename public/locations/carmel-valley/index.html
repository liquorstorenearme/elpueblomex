Mexican Food in Carmel Valley, San Diego

El Pueblo Carmel Valley is our San Diego proper location — tucked into the Village Walk shops at 5965 Village Way, minutes from Pacific Highlands Ranch, Del Mar Heights, and the 56. It's where North County meets the city: a neighborhood restaurant with a full bar, a heated patio, and a menu that runs from early breakfast through late-night dinner every day of the year.

Family-owned, this is the location locals call their go-to. The kitchen is open from 6am to midnight, seven days a week. Breakfast burritos at sunrise. Lunch plates at noon. Margaritas on the patio by 5. Tequila flights at 10pm. The rhythm of Carmel Valley, one table at a time.

Full Bar, Heated Patio, and Private Events

Our Carmel Valley bar is stocked with premium tequila, mezcal, and the house margaritas that have become a neighborhood fixture — silver, gold, spicy, skinny, by the glass or the pitcher. We pour tequila flights (blanco, reposado, añejo side-by-side) and we have a short mezcal list for the curious. Our bartenders know the menu cold, so tell them what you want and they'll match it.

The heated patio is the reason people book birthdays, bridal showers, team dinners, and end-of-season parties with us. It's open year-round — heat lamps in winter, shade and ocean breeze the rest of the year. Private events start at 8 guests; we build the catering menu around your group size.

Fish Tacos, Margaritas, and the Full El Pueblo Menu

Carmel Valley serves the full El Pueblo menu — our signature $1.29 fish tacos (fried-to-order, chipotle sauce, cabbage, pico, no limit), breakfast burritos, carne asada plates, enchiladas, chile rellenos, fajitas, quesadillas, and tortas. Pair any of it with a house margarita, a cold Pacifico, or a mezcal pour; our Carmel Valley regulars lean hard on the spicy margarita.

Frequently asked

Carmel Valley — questions

Where is El Pueblo Carmel Valley?

5965 Village Way, Suite E107, San Diego, CA 92130 — in the Village Walk shops, near the Del Mar Heights / Carmel Valley Road junction off the 56.

Do you have a full bar?

Yes. El Pueblo Carmel Valley has a full bar with premium tequila, mezcal, and craft margaritas. Tequila flights available on request.

Is the patio heated?

Yes. Our heated patio is open year-round and is where most evening dining happens. It also doubles as private-event space for parties of 8 or more.

Can I reserve a table?

Reservations are only available for private events on the patio (8+ guests). Inside dining is first-come, first-served — weekends fill up fast around 6pm.

Are kids welcome?

Yes. Carmel Valley is family-friendly — we have a kids menu, high chairs, and the dining room is open to all ages until close. The bar area is 21+.

Hungry, Carmel Valley?

Order pickup, browse the menu, or drop in — we're ready.
